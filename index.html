Three steps to better love.

  1. Build their profile. Birthday, favourite flowers, the things they secretly love.
  2. Open Cherish when you're stuck. Pick a quick idea, a date night, or a sweet text.
  3. Show up. Cherish tracks what worked so the next gesture lands even better.

Built private by default.

Your partner's profile lives on your device. AI suggestions run on-device through Apple Intelligence — no servers, no analytics, no creepy data trails. Sign in with Apple supports Hide My Email, so even your sign-up address is private if you want.
